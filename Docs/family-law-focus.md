# Family Law Focus

AI OS for Lawyers is especially well suited to family law matters because those matters often involve:

- evolving facts
- high document volume
- chronology disputes
- parenting schedules
- financial disclosure issues
- emergency relief questions
- repeated drafting cycles

## Common Family Law Matter Types

- divorce
- custody
- parenting time
- child support
- spousal support
- property division
- enforcement
- modification
- relocation
- paternity
- protective orders

## Family-Law Workflow Advantages

- structured intake
- document collection discipline
- timeline reconstruction
- issue-by-issue evidence tracking
- hearing and trial preparation support
