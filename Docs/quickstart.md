# Quick Start

## Step 1 — Open the Case Command Center
Create a matter workspace.

## Step 2 — Choose a Matter Template
Select the matter type most closely aligned to the representation.

## Step 3 — Complete the Smart Intake Questionnaire
Capture facts, procedural posture, and requested relief.

## Step 4 — Launch the Client Document Request Portal
Generate the initial document checklist.

## Step 5 — Add Facts, Timeline Events, and Documents
Populate the workspace with matter-specific data.

## Step 6 — Generate Work Product
Use the system for drafting support, issue spotting, evidence tracking, or litigation planning.

## Step 7 — Track What Is Missing
Maintain a live view of missing records, missing facts, deadlines, and next steps.
