# Triage Snapshot Example

## Matter Type
Modification of child custody and parenting schedule

## Procedural Posture
Post-judgment dispute; prior final order exists

## Urgency
Moderate to high; allegations suggest potential immediate parenting conflict

## Verification Status
Mixed; some facts appear documented, others remain client allegations only

## Core Requested Relief
Modification of parenting schedule and clarification of decision-making authority

## Immediate Risk Flags
- possible school-related conflict
- possible noncompliance with existing order
- communication breakdown between parties

## Top Missing Facts
- exact terms of current order
- dates of alleged violations
- school records and communications
- parenting-time logs

## Recommended Immediate Focus
Collect current order, build chronology, identify documented incidents, and confirm whether emergency relief is actually implicated.
