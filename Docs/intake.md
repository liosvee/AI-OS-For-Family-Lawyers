# Smart Intake

The Smart Intake workflow structures client narratives into usable legal intake information.

## Intake Goals

- capture core facts
- identify urgency
- flag safety issues
- surface missing information
- generate attorney follow-up questions
- support downstream document collection

## Common Intake Areas

- party information
- procedural history
- family background
- children
- employment and income
- assets and debts
- prior orders
- current disputes
- requested relief
