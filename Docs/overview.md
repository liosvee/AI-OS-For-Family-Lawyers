# Overview

AI OS for Lawyers is a structured legal operating system designed to help legal teams organize facts, evidence, documents, drafting tasks, deadlines, and strategy in a single workflow-aware environment.

It is designed for attorney-supervised use and should be treated as support infrastructure rather than a substitute for legal judgment.

## Core Objectives

- organize legal matters in a structured workspace
- convert raw intake into usable legal analysis
- surface missing facts and missing records early
- improve consistency across case handling
- accelerate drafting while preserving lawyer review
- support evidence-aware, jurisdiction-sensitive workflows
