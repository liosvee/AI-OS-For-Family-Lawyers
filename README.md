# AI OS for Family Lawyers

https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69b6d656e5488191adda31eb09b091ac-ai-os-for-family-lawyers


**AI OS for Family Lawyers** is a structured AI legal operating system designed to support family law attorneys, legal teams, and family law practices.

It helps organize matters, surface missing facts, structure evidence, accelerate discovery planning, support litigation strategy, and improve day-to-day case execution across family law matters including divorce, custody, parenting time, child support, spousal support, property division, enforcement, modification, relocation, paternity, and protective-order-related disputes.

> Attorney-support work product only. All outputs require lawyer review and jurisdiction-specific validation.

---

## Overview

Family law cases are fact-dense, document-heavy, emotionally charged, and procedurally demanding. AI OS for Family Lawyers is built to function as a centralized operating layer for case intake, case organization, evidence analysis, strategy development, and workflow management.

The system is designed to help legal professionals:

* organize facts, timelines, and issues
* identify evidentiary gaps and missing records
* generate structured discovery and document request lists
* prepare attorney-support strategy work product
* track witnesses, deadlines, and case tasks
* standardize internal workflows across multiple matters

## Safety and Professional Limits

This project is intended for **attorney-support work product only**.

It does **not** replace:

* attorney judgment
* jurisdiction-specific legal research
* court rule verification
* professional responsibility review
* independent evidentiary assessment

The system should not be used to:

* fabricate facts or evidence
* invent statutes, case law, or procedural rules
* hide assets or conceal information
* destroy evidence
* evade court orders
* provide unsupervised legal advice to consumers as though it were counsel of record

Potential safety issues involving children, domestic violence, coercive control, or urgent court-related risk should always be escalated for immediate lawyer review.
